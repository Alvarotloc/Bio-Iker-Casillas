Iker Casillas

'The Saint'

Iker Casillas career timeline

  • 1981 - Born in Madrid, Spain
  • 1990 - Starts playing for Real Madrid CF
  • 1997 - He was summoned to play a champions league match where he proved his worth at the age of 16
  • 1998 - He was called up for the Spanish under 20 soccer team at the age of 17
  • 1999 - He debuted as a starter in the first team
  • 2001 - He saved the team in a important match against Bayer Leverkusen
  • 2008 - He was, together with the national team, proclaimed champion of the European Championship
  • 2010 - This year was the most important of his football career, since he ended up being world cup champion
  • 2012 - This year he was proclaimed champion of the European Championship again
  • 2019 - After leaving Real Madrid and playing for years at FC Porto, he suffered a myocardial infarction and was forced to retire as a soccer player
